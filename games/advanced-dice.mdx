## Roll types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Roll between">
    Win if the roll lands inside a single range. Default 25.00–75.00 (50% win chance). A narrower range yields a higher multiplier.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Roll outside">
    Win if the roll lands outside a single range. A wider exclusion zone yields a higher multiplier.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Roll between two">
    Win if the roll lands inside either of two ranges. Default 25.00–50.00 and 62.50–87.50. Four sliders, two bounds per range.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Multiplier formula

Same as [Dice](/games/dice):

```text
multiplier = (100 - edge) / win_chance
```

`win_chance` is the percentage of the 0–100 range covered by your win zone(s). Multipliers are rounded to four decimals.
