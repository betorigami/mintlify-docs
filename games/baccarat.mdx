## How to play

<Steps>
  <Step title="Place your bet">
    Any combination of Player, Banker, and Tie.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deal">
    Player and Banker each receive two cards. A third may be drawn based on standard rules.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Score">
    Card values: Ace = 1, 2–9 = face value, 10/Face = 0. Only the last digit of the total counts (e.g. 15 scores 5). Closest to 9 wins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Accordion title="Third-card rules">
  ### Player

  | Player's first two cards | Action |
  |:--|:--|
  | 0–5 | Draw |
  | 6–7 | Stand |
  | 8–9 | Natural (both hands stand) |

  ### Banker

  The Banker's draw depends on the Player's third card:

  | Banker total | Draws when Player's 3rd is | Stands when |
  |:--|:--|:--|
  | 0–2 | Always draws | — |
  | 3 | 0–7, 9 | 8 |
  | 4 | 2–7 | 0, 1, 8, 9 |
  | 5 | 4–7 | 0–3, 8, 9 |
  | 6 | 6–7 | 0–5, 8, 9 |
  | 7 | — | Always stands |
  | 8–9 | Natural | Natural |

  If the Player stands on two cards, the Banker draws on 0–5 and stands on 6+.
</Accordion>
