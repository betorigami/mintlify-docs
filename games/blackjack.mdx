## How to play

<Steps>
  <Step title="Place your bet" />
  <Step title="Receive your cards">
    Two face-up cards. The dealer gets one face-up and one face-down.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your action">
    **Hit:** take another card. **Stand:** keep your total. **Double Down:** double your bet and take exactly one more card. **Split:** if your two cards match, play them as two separate hands. Going over 21 is a bust (instant loss).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insurance (optional)">
    If the dealer's face-up card is an Ace, take insurance: a side bet worth half your original bet that pays 2:1 if the dealer has blackjack.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dealer plays">
    Dealer reveals the hole card and draws to 17.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare">
    Closest to 21 without busting wins. Ties push (bet returned).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payouts

| Outcome | Payout |
|:--|:--|
| Natural Blackjack (21 on first two cards) | 2.5x (3:2) |
| Win | 2x (1:1) |
| Insurance pays (dealer has blackjack) | 3x on the insurance bet (2:1) |
| Push (tie) | 1x (bet returned) |
| Loss or Bust | 0x |

## House edge

With basic strategy, the house edge is approximately 0.5% (99.5% RTP). Insurance carries a higher edge.
