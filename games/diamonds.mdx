Five diamonds roll like slot reels. The combination is scored as a poker hand: pair, three of a kind, full house, and so on. Five of a kind pays 50x at every RTP level.

## Paytable

| Hand | 99% | 98% | 97% | 96% | 92% |
|:--|:--|:--|:--|:--|:--|
| Five of a Kind | 50.0x | 50.0x | 50.0x | 50.0x | 50.0x |
| Four of a Kind | 5.0x | 4.8x | 4.6x | 4.4x | 3.6x |
| Full House | 4.3x | 4.0x | 3.7x | 3.4x | 3.2x |
| Three of a Kind | 3.0x | 3.0x | 3.0x | 3.0x | 2.8x |
| Two Pair | 2.0x | 2.0x | 2.0x | 2.0x | 2.0x |
| Pair | 0.1x | 0.1x | 0.1x | 0.1x | 0.1x |
| No Match | 0x | 0x | 0x | 0x | 0x |

Five of a Kind and Two Pair pay the same across all RTP levels. Edge is absorbed through Four of a Kind, Full House, and Three of a Kind.
