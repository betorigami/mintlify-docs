## How to play

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set the target">
    Any value between 0 and 10,000.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the direction">
    Above: win if the roll is higher. Below: win if it's lower.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place your bet">
    The multiplier is calculated from your win chance. Lower chance, higher multiplier.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Multiplier formula

```text
multiplier = (100 - edge) / win_chance
```

`win_chance` is a percentage between 0.01% and 99.99%. Multipliers are rounded to four decimals.
