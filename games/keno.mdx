Pick 1 to 10 numbers on a 40-tile board. Ten tiles are drawn at random. The more matches, the higher the payout. The risk level controls the curve. High pays nothing on partial matches but up to 1,000x on a clean hit.

## How to play

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick your numbers">
    Select 1 to 10 tiles. More picks unlock higher potential multipliers but require more matches.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a risk level">
    Classic: balanced. Low: frequent small returns. Medium: moderate. High: all-or-nothing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play">
    Ten tiles are drawn. Matches against your picks determine your payout.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configuration

Keno-specific artwork:

| Attribute | Description |
|:--|:--|
| `customKenoBackgroundSvgUrl` | Background artwork |
| `customKenoTileSvgUrl` | Default tile |
| `customKenoSelectedTileSvgUrl` | Player-selected tile |
| `customKenoGemSvgUrl` | Matched (gem) tile |
| `customKenoNonMatchTileSvgUrl` | Drawn but unmatched tile |

For shared attributes (edge, limits, branding), see [game settings](/operate/game-settings).
