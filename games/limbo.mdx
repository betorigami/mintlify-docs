Pick a target multiplier. The server generates a random multiplier. If it meets or exceeds your target, you win your target. The higher you aim, the longer the odds and the bigger the payout. Up to 1,000,000x.

## Win-chance formula

```text
win_chance = (100 - edge) / target_multiplier
```

### Example targets at 99% RTP

| Target | Win chance | Implied odds |
|:--|:--|:--|
| 1.01x | 98.0198% | ~1 in 1 |
| 2x | 49.5% | ~1 in 2 |
| 10x | 9.9% | ~1 in 10 |
| 100x | 0.99% | ~1 in 101 |
| 1,000x | 0.099% | ~1 in 1,010 |
| 10,000x | 0.0099% | ~1 in 10,101 |
| 1,000,000x | 0.000099% | ~1 in 1,010,101 |

## RTP scaling

Example at 10x target:

| Edge | RTP | Win chance |
|:--|:--|:--|
| 1% | 99% | 9.9% |
| 2% | 98% | 9.8% |
| 3% | 97% | 9.7% |
| 4% | 96% | 9.6% |
| 8% | 92% | 9.2% |
