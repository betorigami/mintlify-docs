A 5×5 grid hides gems and mines. Reveal tiles one at a time. Each gem raises your multiplier. Hit a mine and the round ends. Cash out at any point to lock in your winnings.

## How to play

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set the number of mines">
    Choose 1–24 mines. More mines means higher multipliers per reveal and higher bust risk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reveal tiles">
    Click tiles one by one. Each safe tile compounds your multiplier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cash out or keep going">
    Cash out anytime. Hit a mine and you lose your bet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Multiplier formula

```text
multiplier = (100 - edge) / win_chance
```

Win chance at each step uses remaining safe tiles vs. total unrevealed tiles. The running multiplier compounds with each reveal.
