## How to play

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose the row count">
    More rows means more pin levels and more buckets. 8 rows gives 9 buckets; 16 rows gives 17.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a risk level">
    Low: frequent small wins with capped upside. Medium: balanced. High: rare top multipliers with lots of near-zero buckets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place your bet and drop">
    The ball bounces through the pins and lands in a bucket. Payout = bet × bucket multiplier.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Paytable

Multipliers shown at 99% RTP. Lower RTP scales them down proportionally. The payout field is symmetric: leftmost and rightmost buckets always match.

## RTP scaling

| Edge | RTP | Effect on multipliers |
|:--|:--|:--|
| 1% | 99% | Full multipliers shown above |
| 2% | 98% | ~1% lower |
| 3% | 97% | ~2% lower |
| 4% | 96% | ~3% lower |
| 8% | 92% | ~7% lower |
