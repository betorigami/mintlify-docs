## Bet types and payouts

### Inside bets

| Bet | Coverage | Payout | Example |
|:--|:--|:--|:--|
| Straight | 1 number | 36x | Bet on 17 |
| Split | 2 adjacent numbers | 18x | 17 / 18 |
| Street | 3 numbers in a row | 12x | 16 / 17 / 18 |
| Corner | 4 numbers in a square | 9x | 16 / 17 / 19 / 20 |
| Double Street | 6 numbers across two rows | 6x | 13–18 |

### Outside bets

| Bet | Coverage | Payout |
|:--|:--|:--|
| Dozen | 12 numbers (1–12, 13–24, 25–36) | 3x |
| Column | 12 numbers (vertical column) | 3x |
| Red / Black | 18 numbers | 2x |
| Even / Odd | 18 numbers | 2x |
| High / Low | 18 numbers (1–18 or 19–36) | 2x |

### Red numbers

1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 12, 14, 16, 18, 19, 21, 23, 25, 27, 30, 32, 34, 36

## House edge

| Variant | Numbers | Zero pockets | House edge | RTP |
|:--|:--|:--|:--|:--|
| European | 37 | 0 | 2.7% | 97.3% |
| American | 38 | 0, 00 | 5.3% | 94.7% |

All bets lose on zero. Payouts are calculated as if zeros don't exist. That's where the edge comes from.
