## Paytable

Each segment maps to a multiplier tier (A–E) or a zero. ZERO segments pay 0x.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Low risk">
    | Segment | Multiplier |
    |:--|:--|
    | A (green) | 1.2x |
    | B (yellow) | 1.5x |
    | ZERO (black) | 0x |

    Repeating layout per 10 segments: `B, A, A, A, ZERO, A, A, A, A, ZERO`.

    | Segments | ZEROs | A | B | Win chance |
    |:--|:--|:--|:--|:--|
    | 10 | 2 | 7 | 1 | 80% |
    | 20 | 4 | 14 | 2 | 80% |
    | 30 | 6 | 21 | 3 | 80% |
    | 40 | 8 | 28 | 4 | 80% |
    | 50 | 10 | 35 | 5 | 80% |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Medium risk">
    | Segment | 10 seg | 20 seg | 30 seg | 40 seg | 50 seg |
    |:--|:--|:--|:--|:--|:--|
    | A (green) | 1.5x | 1.5x | 1.5x | 1.5x | 1.5x |
    | B (yellow) | 1.9x | 1.8x | 1.7x | 1.6x | 2x |
    | C (blue) | 2x | 2x | 2x | 2x | 3x |
    | D (red) | 3x | 3x | 3x | 3x | 5x |
    | E (purple) | — | — | 4x | — | — |
    | ZERO (black) | 0x | 0x | 0x | 0x | 0x |

    | Segments | ZEROs | A | B | C | D | E |
    |:--|:--|:--|:--|:--|:--|:--|
    | 10 | 5 | 2 | 1 | 1 | 1 | — |
    | 20 | 10 | 2 | 1 | 6 | 1 | — |
    | 30 | 15 | 6 | 1 | 6 | 1 | 1 |
    | 40 | 20 | 8 | 1 | 7 | 4 | — |
    | 50 | 25 | 13 | 8 | 3 | 1 | — |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="High risk">
    High risk has exactly one winning segment. Every other segment is ZERO.

    | Segments | Multiplier | Win chance |
    |:--|:--|:--|
    | 10 | 9.9x | 10% |
    | 20 | 19.8x | 5% |
    | 30 | 29.7x | 3.3% |
    | 40 | 39.6x | 2.5% |
    | 50 | 49.5x | 2% |

    Formula: `multiplier = segments × (1 - edge)`. At 99% RTP, `segments × 0.99`.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Configuration

Wheel-specific artwork:

| Attribute | Description |
|:--|:--|
| `customWheelBackgroundSvgUrl` | Background artwork |
| `customWheelCenterSvgUrl` | Wheel-center artwork |
| `customWheelCenterPadding` | Padding around the center image |

For shared attributes (edge, limits, branding), see [game settings](/operate/game-settings).
