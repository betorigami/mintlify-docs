## Launch path

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a delivery path">
    Connect through [Hub88 or SoftSwiss](/operate/integration-options) if you already use an aggregator, or go direct for the tightest control.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your games">
    Set edge, limits, and any per-market overrides. See [game settings](/operate/game-settings) for the full list of attributes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply your brand">
    Drop in your [logo, artwork, and themes](/operate/branding). Every game launches looking native to your platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test end to end">
    Validate auth, balances, limits, and round settlement before launch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Ship to production. New titles and improvements roll out to your integration automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Demo mode

Every Origami game supports demo mode for pre-launch integration testing and end-user previews. Demo sessions run against a virtual balance that can be exhausted during play.

The [game pages](/games/catalog) in these docs embed live demo instances you can play in the browser, no setup required.

Contact [sales@betorigami.com](mailto:sales@betorigami.com) to get a demo environment provisioned for your platform.
